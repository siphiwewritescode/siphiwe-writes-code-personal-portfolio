About

As a BI Developer and Data Engineer, I specialise in turning complex, fragmented data into clear, actionable intelligence. My work spans dashboard development, ETL pipeline design, data modelling and cross-functional stakeholder engagement. I help businesses make faster, more confident decisions.

'Data isn't just numbers — it's the story a business doesn't know it's telling yet.'

With a background that bridges analytical rigour and technical execution, I have worked across industries including automotive and consumer goods, building reporting systems, resolving data quality issues and delivering insights at scale across multiple countries and teams.

I have built and worked with AI-powered workflows and agentic systems, leveraging tools like Claude Code and Codex to push the boundaries of what data professionals can build independently. When I am not deep in data, I run a software and technology venture, always solving the next problem worth solving.
